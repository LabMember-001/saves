After getting the key to King Mammon's imprisonment from Shannon, I died in
the battle so that I could go back to Brannoch to save.

This file supports all releases of Holy Magic Century (PAL) and Quest 64
(NTSC, USA) but not the Japanese release because of some strong differences.

HP and MP are both 500:500.
Defense and Agility are both maxed at 255:255.

All of Celtland's spirits are collected (with the obvious need of hacking the
save binary), so you can cast any spell now.

A lot of recovery or other battle-use items are stocked up in the inventory.
